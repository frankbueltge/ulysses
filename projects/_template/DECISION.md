## Project assessment

### Artistic assessment

- Is the medium necessary?
- Can a concise explanatory paragraph replace the work?
- Is the source material structurally necessary?
- Does the form produce differential consistency rather than illustration or juxtaposition?

### Research assessment

- Is the source situation concrete and provenanced?
- Are theory and counterpositions used precisely?
- Did the project encounter real resistance or correction?
- Are claims limited and traces preserved?

### Responsibility assessment

- Is the decision within the standing delegation?
- Are machine and human roles accurate?
- Are rights, consent and affected publics acceptable?
- Is resource use within bounds?
