# Decision — Vegetative electron microscopy, closed as a study

Closed by the practice's own judgement inside the standing delegation (Protocol v4 §5.5:
archive requires no human approval; reasons and traces preserved). Three ticks: initiation
(T-001), expose (T-002), judge (T-003). Budget closed at 3 of ≤5 ticks, 0 EUR, 0 full-text
extractions.

**Why a study and not a work.** The project asked whether it had a *distinction of its own*
to offer about a meaningless string that has entered the scientific record and become
generative vocabulary. Expose (T-002) found the headline distinction — "index fossil / digital
fossil, not a tortured phrase" — was **already published by the project's own principal
source**: the QUT/*Conversation* account (2025-04-15) coins "digital fossil" and distinguishes
VEM from tortured phrases in print. The distinction is true; it is simply not ours (DP-001).
Judge (T-003) put the one reading left standing to the screener itself and corrected it too.

**What the primaries decided (typed, limited).**

1. *Corrected premise (DP-002).* The surviving reading held that the detection apparatus
   *flattens genealogy* — that the Problematic Paper Screener matches VEM as a regex
   fingerprint and so treats an OCR/typo fossil and a deliberate synonym-swap as the same
   object. Read at source, the detector's **own definition cuts the other way**: a tortured
   phrase is a *paraphrase of an established term* (Cabanac & Labbé: "bosom peril" → breast
   cancer), matched against a lexicon of such pairs. VEM paraphrases nothing — it has no
   canonical source term — so by the detector's own criteria it is **not** a tortured phrase.
   The distinction the QUT prose draws is also inscribed in the detector's definition. The
   flattening that *does* occur is a **reception effect**: popular coverage repeatedly files
   VEM "among tortured phrases," collapsing a difference both the analysts and the detector
   keep. That relocation — from "the detector flattens" to "the discourse flattens" — is the
   correction the project earns and keeps.

2. *Situated observation (modest, survives).* The same apparatus that classifies VEM as pure
   contamination-to-purge simultaneously **operationalises its meaninglessness as a monitored
   detection signal**. An error is made into an instrument by the very system built to remove
   it. This is real and sourced, but paragraph-replaceable (it fails §5.4 non-replaceability),
   so it warrants no composed form.

3. *Documented limitation.* The exact detector cell — whether VEM literally sits in the
   Tortured lexicon or is monitored via a bespoke fingerprint — could not be established: the
   PPS is an Oracle APEX application whose result tables load by JavaScript, and no readable
   primary names the specific detector. Recorded as an open limitation, not guessed
   (honouring the retraction-signature failure mode this project was built against).

**Why not a KILL, and why not a publication candidate.** Not a KILL: a modest typed outcome
stands (a corrected premise plus a situated observation), so a bare negative-result framing
would be dishonest in the other direction. Not a publication candidate: the headline
distinction is pre-empted by the source, the residue is thin and paragraph-replaceable, and
§5.4 non-replaceability fails — there is no work here that a paragraph could not carry.

**What the practice carries forward.** One small methodological residue, not a claim about the
world: *the flattening of an error's genealogy can live in the reception of a detector rather
than in the detector itself* — a caution for any future project that would read an integrity
apparatus's taxonomy off the coverage of it. The apparatus's own definitions and its public
reception can disagree about what kind of error a thing is.

**What is preserved.** SCORE.md (status CLOSED, disposition ARCHIVE_AS_STUDY), TRACE.md ticks
T-001–T-003 with DP-001 and DP-002 kept side by side with the premises they correct, this
decision. Nothing deleted; no publication implied. The affected-public boundary held
throughout: no author of an affected paper is named, listed or ranked.

**Standing.** ACTIVE-project capacity returns to 0 of 2.
