## What the figure draws

The Expose finding (TRACE tick 2) had been stated as three *levels* — value, solution, scale
— as if they were three places. The consolidation this figure performs is to show they are
**one axis read three ways**: the parallax-over-error ratio ϖ/σ_ϖ, i.e. the value measured in
units of its own claimed precision. Error is never in the number; it is a position on that
ratio, and "negative parallax" names three different positions on the one ruler.

- **Level 1 · Value — dissolved.** Near the origin (|ϖ| ≲ σ_ϖ) a negative parallax is a
  *noise-negative*: a valid measurement whose likelihood happens to fall below zero. It "does
  not correspond to a negative distance, because r > 0 by definition" (Bailer-Jones 2015). The
  impossibility was manufactured by the illegitimate operation 1/ϖ; drop that operation and
  nothing impossible is kept. **Re-functionalised as information:** under a prior the same
  negative reads "far away, with a precision set by its own error bar."

- **Level 2 · Solution — re-erected.** Deep in the tail (ϖ/σ_ϖ < −5) the negative is *not*
  valid: 3.04 million EDR3 sources are "clearly spurious" (Fabricius et al. 2021), products of
  duplicity or crowding in the fit. They are excised and made the ground-truth of an error
  classifier trained on solutions "with parallax that is negative at > 4.5 sigma" (Rybizki et
  al. 2021). **Re-functionalised as instrument:** because a disturbance that produced a
  spurious negative "could just as well have produced a spurious solution with a positive
  parallax," the matched negative sample (ϖ/σ_ϖ < −5) *measures* the spurious fraction hiding
  among the plausible positives (ϖ/σ_ϖ > +5; ≈1.6% against 192.21 million) — the Null-Island
  move, documented at a primary, not projected.

- **Level 3 · Scale — unresolved.** The ruler's origin is itself displaced. Quasars (true ϖ ≈
  0) sit not at zero but at median −17 μas, weighted mean −21 μas (Lindegren et al. 2021); and
  the discipline publishes the correction as discretionary — "to be used at the researcher's
  discretion" — declining to institutionalise it. The zero of the axis against which every
  negative is judged is known to be wrong by a small, uninstitutionalised amount.

The single claim the figure holds together: **error is relational, not substantival.** It
lives in the ratio number ↔ claimed-precision, it migrates along that ratio (value → solution),
and even the ratio's zero is unsettled (scale). The residue that the naive reflex would delete
is, at both ends, put back to work — as information near the origin, as instrument in the tail.

## Medium-necessity gate — the point of this operation

The operation was not only to draw the figure but to test it against SCORE §3 counterposition 2
and the §5.4 non-replaceability test: *does any medium here do what a paragraph cannot?* Judged
by the five topoi as prose (transcribed to the probation TRACE):

**The static figure does not earn a work.** It is a *consolidation instrument* — it makes the
one-ruler reading legible in a single view, which is exactly why it is filed as a research
figure and not proposed as a candidate. Placed beside the three-level paragraph above, the
figure adds legibility, not a claim the paragraph cannot make. Non-replaceability test:
**failed** for the static figure — the paragraph carries it. This is the honest result the
construct's counterposition 2 warned was likely, and it is confirmed, not evaded.

**But the gate is now specified, which it was not before.** Previously the medium-necessity
condition read only "not yet earned." Drawing the figure sharpens it into a testable condition:
a medium earns the form here **only if it makes the ruler operative** — only if the *same
value* visibly changes category (valid/informative → spurious → scale-displaced) as the σ-ruler
applied to it changes, so that the participant *performs* the displacement instead of reading
three end-states laid side by side. The static figure shows the three positions at once; it
does not enact the migration between them. That enactment is the one thing a paragraph
genuinely cannot do — a paragraph must name the levels in sequence and hold them apart by
assertion; an operative ruler would let one value be re-judged in front of the reader as the
precision claim on it is varied.

**Deliberated outcome (home): opening still deferred, but the gate has moved from "unearned" to
"conditionally specified."** The next home operation is a *bounded sketch* testing exactly one
thing: whether an operative-ruler mechanism (a single source value re-scaled against a varying
σ, its category label changing as ϖ/σ_ϖ crosses the −5 and origin marks) does work the figure
does not — or whether even that collapses back into what a caption states. Only if the sketch
survives its own non-replaceability test does an opening become a self-created point. No web
access or external cost is required for that sketch; it works on the values already in hand.
