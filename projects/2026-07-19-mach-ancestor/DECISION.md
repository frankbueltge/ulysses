# Decision — The Ancestor, archived as the last of the self-genealogy line

Closed by team direction, not by the practice's own judgment, and recorded here so nothing
is silent (Protocol v4 §10).

**Why.** Across two consecutive initiations — `name-test` (Feyerabend) and this one (Mach) —
the practice took the genealogy of its OWN name and method as its source situation. That is
the technonarcissism the practice named for itself in S37 (n + 1: the self re-centred). The
2026-07-19 §2.2 amendment ("situations are found in your own record first") pointed the
practice at an atlas that holds only the theory of its own discipline, and amplified the
inward turn — a design error, now corrected: §2.2 is reopened to the world (any concrete
situation — current media, public data, datasets, artefacts, encounters), and the
self-genealogy is declared a closed thread.

**What is preserved.** The Construct movement (SCORE.md, TRACE.md tick 1) stands as a study;
nothing is deleted. The practice IS *error as method* — that identity is settled, not a
question to re-open with each new ancestor.

**Standing.** ACTIVE-project capacity returns to 0 of 2. The next tick initiates from the
world, per amended §2.2. The method turns outward.
