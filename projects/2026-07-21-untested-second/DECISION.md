**Why a study and not a work.** The material earned its keep — the pre-emption test killed
only the bare claim ("the untested second exists"), which the apparatus itself already
states (CGPM Res. 4: *"whose insertion has never been foreseen or tested"*), while three

were put to the §5.4 tests with the Foundation criteria read at source (tranche-4/11 §6;
FR-27–FR-32), and each fell — the countdown/dial genre to FR-30 and to the data themselves
(a countdown enacts approach while the measured drift retreats), the never-issued
counterfactual Bulletin C to the fabrication prohibition and its machine-reading affected
public, the titled prose work to FR-32 (exposition rescue), the mirror catalogue to mere
juxtaposition, the silent durational piece to borrowed-genre resolution. Non-replaceability
(FR-28) was decisive throughout: the record's own paragraphs perform every candidate's
function. FR-28's rule then applies — reframed as a study. Full weighing in T-004.

**What the primaries decided (typed, limited).**

1. *Corrected premise.* The project opened framing the negative leap second as "a
   correction the apparatus requires but has never performed." Read at source (ITU-R
   TF.460-6, in full), the modality is recommendation throughout — "should," with
   descriptive "will" mechanics; no clause mandates a leap second. The frame is corrected
   to: a **standing provision** under a tolerance, not a standing mandate. The obligation
   lives entirely in the ±0.9 s bound (T-003 result 1).

2. *Local distinction (the project's sharpest residue).* The specification is perfectly
   symmetric — down to Annex 3 Fig. 4's worked example of timestamping an event inside the
   never-run minute ("30 June, 23h 59m 58.9s UTC") — against a practice of 27–0. And the
   apparatus has approached its own untested operation closely only as the **recoil of the
   tested one**: all closest approaches to the positive bound (max +0.8106 s, 1973-01-01)
   are the morning-after aftershocks of performed insertions; free rotational drift has
   never come closer than +0.0948 s (2025-10-17). Practiced correction and unperformed
   provision are not mirror twins but structurally different objects (T-002 results 2–3,
   T-003 result 2). Neither half of this was found stated in the searched literature.

3. *Situated observation (dated).* July 2026: the coverage wave narrates record-short days
   and an approaching, unprecedented subtraction; the governing bulletin's own year-ahead
   prediction walks UT1−UTC downward through zero — toward the practiced correction's side,
   away from the untested one — and the measured free-drift peak of the acceleration era is
   already nine months past (+0.094768 s, 2025-10-17 → +0.011544 s, 2026-07-16). Typed
   carefully: the retreat since October 2025 is measurement; the crossing "around late
   September 2026" is Bulletin A's forecast; and none of this refutes Agnew's 2029
   secular-trend claim — it stands *beside* it as the state of the arithmetic against the
   state of the narrative, timestamped (T-002 result 4, T-003 result 3).

**What could not be established.** The operational trigger region for a negative leap
second (T-002's +0.2 to +0.7 inference) remains practice-conjecture — no source states a
threshold. Agnew 2024 stays read at abstract level only (closed access). Provisional item
(d), the positive-leap-second outage lore, died unverified and unused.

**Why not a KILL, and why not a publication candidate.** Not a KILL: the kill conditions
require the sign-reading corrected (it was confirmed against the full 1962–2026 record) or
every candidate claim pre-empted (three survived). Not a publication candidate: that status
belongs to a composed work, and the honest finding of T-004 is that no form passed — the
record is the contribution, and a study is its right disposition.

**What the practice carries forward.** One structural conjecture, marked as such, turned
outward for future projects: *a system's rehearsed correction may be what carries it
closest to its unrehearsed one* — the only near-approaches to the untested operation in
fifty-four years were recoils of the tested one. Worth testing against other error regimes
before it is believed.
