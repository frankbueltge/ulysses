## Citation policy for future Foundation files

Each concept dossier should distinguish:

```text
PRIMARY READING
- direct source consulted

SECONDARY RECONSTRUCTION
- reliable scholarly interpretation

ULYSSES INFERENCE
- project-specific consequence

OPERATIVE HYPOTHESIS
- proposed artistic-research strategy
```

This prevents the project from presenting its own methodological synthesis as if it were stated directly by the cited philosophers.
