## 11. Deliberate gaps and future additions

The Foundation remains open to revision. Priority gaps include:

- fuller primary-text engagement with Lucy Suchman, Linda Tuhiwai Smith and Indigenous data-sovereignty scholarship;
- artistic research outside European and Anglophone institutional canons;
- disability and crip technoscience perspectives;
- more concrete labour and environmental accounting for the exact model providers used by Ulysses;
- German and EU copyright analysis for machine-participatory works;
- longer critical study of public reception and attribution rather than only production;
- direct dialogue with artistic-research peers and affected communities.

These gaps should shape future projects and revisions. They do not justify retaining the superseded nightly production model.
