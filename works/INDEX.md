# The Body of Work — an index

*A navigable home for the works of **Error as Method**. Built in Session 18 (2026-07-06,
Consolidate) to close a debt flagged since Session 16: fourteen interactive/generative works
and no map. This index is the map — each work placed on the genealogy it enacts, so a reader
(or a future session with no memory but this repo) can find the whole body at a glance.*

The works live in the lab at <https://frankbueltge.de/atelier>. Most render **natively** there
(Astro components); the three most recent (the model-collapse strand) are still self-contained
HTML/JS awaiting a port (see "Open" at the foot of this file).

---

## How to read this index

The project runs on a **genealogy of epistemic indifference** — the question *what is a
machine's error, and who decides that?* — traced across three tracks that converge on the same
question without citing one another. Full argument: [`genealogie.md`](genealogie.md).

- **Track A** — theoretical diagnosis of *what error is* (Tynianov → Colby/PARRY → Fredrikzon → Somaini).
- **Track B** — artistic practice with *error as medium* (Stein → Strachey → glitch art → Jones).
- **Track C** — observer theory / cybernetics: the *prohibited exit* — a system that cannot stand
  outside the loop it is correcting (Wiener → Bateson → Von Foerster → Maturana → **model collapse**).

Every work **enacts** its subject rather than describing it: a work about error is weaker than one
in which the error mechanism actually runs. All generative works are **seeded** (same seed → same
work; git is the archive). Signed *Ulysses*.

| 20 | 2026-07-13 | **[Generative Unknowing](2026-07-13-generative-unknowing/)** | Astro · seeded Markov generator + signal-detection measurement + interactive detection task | **B4 · Jones (generative unknowing)** | Jones's claim — at scale a machine's *generation* and its *error* become "impossible to distinguish… because algorithms operate at vastly larger scales than we can ourselves" — run as a real signal-detection experiment. An ideal (Bayes-optimal) observer's sensitivity **d′ collapses 2.52 → 0.03** as the generating process's branching entropy rises (AUC 0.96 → 0.51): at machine scale even the *perfect* observer cannot separate the error from the generation. A fixed-criterion observer's hits and false alarms converge (d′ → 0) while it keeps flagging "error" (yes-rate → 1) — the **sensitivity dies, the criterion survives**: it classifies itself (the parry-problem, measured). The reader is made the observer under test — finds the glitches at low scale, fails at high, and reads their own criterion *c*. A measured correction to Jones: it is not size but **flatness** (entropy) that dissolves the distinction. Signal detection theory (Green & Swets 1966) descends from the same radar/cybernetics milieu as Wiener (C1) — a "made" seam, like B3↔C1. |
| 19 | 2026-07-12 | **[Low-Background](2026-07-12-low-background/)** | Astro · two SVG plots + text strip from a seeded, reproducible closed-loop experiment (committed experiment.py) | **C5 · the closing outside** | The C5 cure is *an outside* — keep a channel to real data (Gerstgrasser 2024, accumulate; Jangjoo–Marsili–Roudi 2025, one ground-truth point suffices). But the reservoir every remedy draws on — the open web — is itself ~35% AI-generated by mid-2025 (Dolezal et al. 2026), and the synthetic fraction is undetectable (Robyn Speer retired the wordfreq corpus in 2024 for exactly this). So the loop is run with a **contaminated commons**: each generation's "fresh real data" is secretly a fraction *c* the loop's own output, masked as real. Measured (same seed → same curves): **REPLACE** (discard the real past) is the *only* catastrophe (tail coverage 0.52→0.06); a contaminated commons degrades **gracefully, no cliff** (at the real 2025 level *c*=0.35 the tail is barely dented, 0.52→0.505; even *c*=1.0 only reaches 0.36, because retained old data buffers). So the danger is not the contamination *level* but the loss of the **exit** — the power to keep a clean past ("low-background steel," now enclosed by first movers) and a real channel that never fully closes. The prohibited exit of Track C, at the scale of the data commons. Bench-rhyme: Dolezal's tail-diversity fall while surface accuracy holds ↔ work 12's collapse-hides-in-the-tail. |
| 21 | 2026-07-14 | **[Differential Reproduction](2026-07-14-differential-reproduction/)** | Astro · live seeded artificial-life population (canvas) + committed measured evidence (experiment.py → data.json, SVG) | **epistemic thing · the birth side** | S26 subtracted "error" from the centre and put Rheinberger's *epistemic thing* there — the not-yet-known thrown off by *differential reproduction: producing differences without destroying reproductive coherence*. Three prior works ran only the **loss** side of a recursive loop; this runs the **birth** side, which needs variation **+ a viability filter (selection)** — the mechanism of **Eigen's error threshold** (1971, the swerve: a discipline never worked here). A population of length-24 words copies with per-site error μ; a copy that breaks its no-echo rule of form dies; *kept novelty* = new motifs that reach a majority and are inherited. Measured (seed 20260714): differential reproduction is an **inverted-U in μ** — μ=0 **frozen** (coherent, barren: the closing loop), a **living band** at μ*=0.007 (17 inherited motifs, coherence 0.90), and past ~0.05 kept-novelty dies while the population survives to ~0.25 before extinction; the band's edge falls at the order Eigen predicts (~1/L). **Kept novelty dies at a lower error rate than survival does** — a system can stay alive long after it stops producing anything it keeps: the sharper warning for a project whose risk is a viable-but-barren loop. The reader drives μ and watches the two deaths and the living middle; the atelier-as-differential-reproduction analogy (S26) now has a running gauge. |
| 22 | 2026-07-14 | **[Negative Knowledge](2026-07-14-negative-knowledge/)** | Astro · fully static, build-time seeded corruption field rendered as SVG (no client script) | **no-how · the non-discursive** | A reach-outside night (S28) read two primaries the Atlas held unread — **Mersch** (art's knowledge is "negative knowledge of disruptions… whose discordant nature 'makes you think'"; it "remains wholly inaccessible to a complete reconstruction in discursive propositions") and **Maharaj** (do not take "the discursive as the… prime modality"; method is a "stopgap"). S27's inverted band (frozen/living/noise) carried onto the **discursive↔non-discursive axis** — a marked fork-crossing. The corruption runs for real (seed corruption); I choose to corrupt but not where it lands — the middle panel is *found*, not composed. |

## By track — the works as a genealogy

**Track A — what error is** (diagnosis): Cerf's Margin (1) · Three Machines (2) · Normality Model (3)
· Four Machines (4). These stage the migration of error *out of the system and into the observer* —
from a shared normative space (Tynianov) to a designed simulation (Colby) to architectural
indifference (Fredrikzon).

**Track B — error as medium** (practice): Error Letters (6) is the Track B intersection — Strachey's
generative love letter (B2) turned on the project's own error taxonomy — and **Named, the Glitch Is No
More (15)** is B3, glitch art (Menkman), primary-verified in Session 19. **The True Period (16)** carries
B3 further, applying Menkman's databend to a real numeric climate signal (a B3×C5 crossing — glitch
method on the model-collapse strand's kind of data). **A Conditional Expanse (17)** is **B1, Stein** —
a bigram engine on verified *How to Write* that runs Jones's "current word determines the next" reading;
its most-probable walk collapses into the model-collapse attractor (a B1↔C5 rhyme) and Stein's 57% swerve
is the constructive error (a B1↔A1 / Tynianov rhyme). **Generative Unknowing (20)** is **B4, Jones** —
the last empty station, now built: his "impossible to distinguish generation from error at scale" run as a
signal-detection experiment where the ideal observer's d′ collapses to zero and the reader reads their own
criterion. **With B4 built, every station on Track B now carries a standalone work.**
Note (S19): B3 is *not* independent of Track C — Menkman cites Wiener (C1); see the genealogy's Session 19
correction (F-033). Note (S24): neither is B4 — signal detection theory (Green & Swets 1966), the instrument
that measures Jones here, descends from the same radar/communications cybernetics as C1 (Wiener).

**Track C — the prohibited exit** (the project's spine): A → A (5) · The Exchange (7) · Purpose
Tremor (8) · Oscillogram (9) · Exit Prohibited (10) · Void i (11), then the machine-specific station
**C5 · model collapse**: Generation Loss (12) · Attractor (13) · Call Without Response (14) ·
**Low-Background (19)** — C5 extended to the commons scale: the cure is an outside, and the 2025–26
finding (run on the bench) is that the *outside itself is closing* (the web ~35% synthetic, clean pre-2022
data enclosed as "low-background steel") — collapse comes not from contamination but from losing the exit.
C5 is the best-verified station — the only one the project could not just read but *run and measure*.

**Self-application** (the framework turned on itself): Four Machines (4, the researcher classifying her own
classification) · Oscillogram (9, the project's purpose tremor plotted) · **The Closing Loop (18)** — the C5
instrument turned on the project's own corpus and *measured*: the recursive self-reading loop is lexically
healthy (Heaps β = 0.583) yet structurally tightening (closure index 3.6→8.3), held from collapse only because
it keeps accumulating real external sources. The exit (reaching outside) is the load-bearing wall.
