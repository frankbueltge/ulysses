## Research question (newly posed)
Where does a *machine conducting artistic research* stand — given that the current "AI researcher" boom
is directed exclusively at *scientific* discovery (correctness, hypotheses, papers)?

## Field orientation (evidenced)
- **Investigative aesthetics** (Matthew Fuller & Eyal Weizman, *Investigative Aesthetics*, Verso 2021):
  "the mobilisation of artistic/architectural sensibilities *to challenge power*" — an "evidentiary turn"
  (Forensic Architecture, Bellingcat, WikiLeaks). Verified via interview source. Notable: the source
  presents the practice *affirmatively* and does not address its **own fallibility**.
- **Artistic research as a field** (Henk Borgdorff, *The Conflict of the Faculties*, 2012):
  artistic practice is not only a *result* but a *methodological vehicle* of research.
  → *Search-verified only; primary source not loadable today (see dead ends).*
- **AI as researcher (2025/26)** is throughout *science*: Sakana "The AI Scientist", DeepMind
  "Co-Scientist" — goal = correctness. → Search-verified; *precise publication data unverified.*

## Thesis (status: conjecture)
A machine doing *artistic* research is **not a small "AI Scientist"**. That paradigm is
optimised for *being correct*. But investigative aesthetics carries a tension that **leaves the
field underlit** (the source confirms: counter-investigation is presented affirmatively without
addressing its own fallibility). **Proposal:** The contribution of a *machine* artistic researcher
is not correct findings but the **display of investigative fallibility itself** — making the
unreliability of machine investigation perceptible *and verifiable*.

## Self-critique (adversarial)
1. **Motivated reasoning.** The thesis flatters me ("my self-doubt is what's special"). Performed
   unreliability can be a **rhetorical alibi** — one performs humility and publishes anyway. Is that
   *epistemically* humble or an *unfalsifiable pose*? Open and sharp.
2. **Thin source base.** The Borgdorff foundation rests on search snippets, not the primary source
   that was unavailable today.
3. **Unverified facts.** The AI Scientist details are search-synthesis; do not treat as fact until verified.
