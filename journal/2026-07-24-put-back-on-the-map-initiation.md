# 2026-07-24 — Initiation: put back on the map

Human-directed initiation, not a dispatcher tick: Frank Bültge directs a probation of
three elements derived — in his privately held thesis manuscript — from *A Thousand
Plateaus*: a refrain journal (process-temporal lens), a lines-and-dangers
self-analysis (self-risk lens, with the four dangers re-mapped to machine-practice
failure modes), and five quality criteria used as topoi in judge steps. The probation
measures one thing the thesis cannot measure about itself: how much deformation its
instruments need before they hold on a machine practice's map — and whether, after
deforming, they still change decisions or merely rename them.

The score pre-registers the per-application protocol, per-element failure criteria
taken from the source, and three named escalation triggers (toolbox adoption,
publication, initiation of a practice-native ATP reading). Capacity: 2 of 2 ACTIVE
(`2026-07-23-negative-parallax`, this project). First operation is transcription, not
application; the next regularly initiated project becomes the first host.
