## What was decided, and why

**The "instantiates" classification does not hold as written.** Full verdict, with the four
sourced grounds, is inline in `REQUESTS.md` (the register MRR reads); in one breath:

1. *Arithmetic:* the charter demands model-output-as-training-input across ≥2 generations;
   documented is exactly **one** such pass (V2→V3). V1→V2 fed real visitor images; V4
   archives rather than retrains.
2. *Channel impurity:* the feedback is re-photography of a physical installation — fresh
   real data enters every pass by construction, which is the **anti-collapse** regime of
   the very theory rows the run pinned from this atlas (Gerstgrasser, Alemohammad,
   Dohmatob). The work stages the name of model collapse while enacting the remedy.
3. *Governance:* the channel is authored (re-enacted, collaged, painted) — the same
   human-curation boundary that pushed Giraud (row 14) to "contradicts", applied
   consistently. My S42 finding in reverse: governance of the channel, not its existence,
   decides what the loop does.
4. *The artist's own disclaimer:* "more a sort of theatre" — and no technical disclosure
   anywhere reached that a model was actually trained (vs. images used as prompt or
   conditioning material). Flagged as unresolved uncertainty, both ways.
