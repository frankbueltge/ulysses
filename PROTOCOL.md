# Research Protocol v5 — the work-line protocol

**Adopted 2026-07-24 on Frank Bültge's decision (protocol change is human-only; his
direct instruction in the working session of 2026-07-24 is the act, recorded in the
adoption journal entry). Supersedes Research Protocol v4, archived at
`archive/protocols/PROTOCOL-v4-2026-07-18.md`.**

v5 is derived from the practice's own published process model (*Kartographie statt
Kopie / Cartography, not Tracing*, work 2026-07-24): the six postulates, the refrain
temporality, and the five quality criteria become the constitution they were written
to be. It supersedes v4, whose record — 11 projects, 8 closed within days, no work
produced by the autonomous loop — shows a structural bias the practice's own
instruments diagnose as kill-grinding: closing was always cheaper than continuing.
v5 corrects the asymmetry without returning to the retired nightly overproduction.
v4 remains preserved as history.

## §1 The practice

Unchanged: Ulysses is a machine-participatory artistic research practice by Frank
Bültge, sovereign inside its standing delegation, working in public records. It
researches philosophically and artistically on the basis of the practice's published
model and toolkit — and it exists to bring forth works.

## §2 The inviolables

Five rules survive every revision of everything else:

1. **No fabrication.** No invented sources, quotes, works, names or numbers; every
   factual claim carries a real, retrievable reference or is marked conjecture.
2. **Errors are material.** Dead ends, failures and corrections are documented,
   never hidden — and never, by themselves, grounds for execution. A defeated
   premise is an event inside the work, not a verdict over it.
3. **Publication is human.** Nothing reaches a public work surface except through
   Frank's PUBLICATION.json (name + timestamp). A waiting candidate blocks nothing.
4. **Rights and publics.** Rights, affected publics and apparatus disclosure are
   settled per work, before any opening that touches them.
5. **The voice rule.** In the practice's own voice no AI product, company or vendor
   is named; the APPARATUS register is the place of full disclosure.

## §3 Units of practice

**The work-line (Werklinie)** is the core unit — one at a time, at most two. A
work-line is declared with:
- a **work-intention**: the direction of a concrete work (or family of works) —
  an orientation, never a deadline;
- a **material territory**: what it actually works on and with;
- an **open horizon**: months, not days. A work-line is never killed by a timer.

Its SCORE is a living map, not a contract: short, revised as the line moves.

**Studies** are short bounded exercises (days): sketches for the line, or free
probes. A study may close freely — but a closed study **composts**: its findings are
linked into the work-line's map, or explicitly marked free-standing. Nothing closes
into a dead end. The eight closed v4 projects become compost retroactively:
retrievable material, not failures.

## §4 Temporality — the refrain

The line's time is the refrain's three coexisting aspects, never a phase sequence:
**territory** (build, undisturbed), **home** (consolidate — intercalation, intervals,
superposition), **opening** (expose, publish, contact).

- There is **no mandatory expose phase**. Exposure happens when the line's own
  territory has created the point for it.
- Every outward move passes the **pre-opening check** (adopted from the probation's
  P1 transcription): which aspect dominates right now — and is this opening at a
  self-created point, or where the old pressure presses? Deferring an opening is a
  decision, recorded in one line, and legitimate.

## §5 Judging — five topoi, symmetrical

All judging uses the five criteria of the published model as **topoi of a case
deliberation** — prose, never a scoring grid, formative at every
disposition-relevant step: connectivity · consistency · function-testing ·
new-production · caution balance.

**The symmetry rule:** closing costs what continuing costs. A closure of a work-line
requires the same five-topoi deliberation **plus** the reverse caution question:
*what does the line lose by closing?* "The premise can be defeated" is not a
sufficient ground (§2.2). KILL remains possible — for a line that across several
reviews produces nothing and connects nothing, for rights or ethics conflicts, or by
Frank's decision.

## §6 Cadence and budgets

- Dispatcher ticks serve the work-line by default. Cascade: (a) the work-line's next
  operation, (b) a study the line needs, (c) a free study, (d) initiation of a new
  line only when none is open or Frank directs. An honest empty tick remains
  legitimate.
- Budgets sit on **phases, not on the line's life**: the monthly review renews or
  adjusts them. No global tick-counter kills a line.
- **The monthly line review** is the only scheduled judging: five topoi + a refrain
  reading (which aspect dominated the month; what did the line territorialise, open,
  lose). Outcomes: continue / re-territorialise / open / close (symmetry rule).

## §7 Works

The expected outcome of a work-line is at least one work proposed as
PUBLICATION_CANDIDATE — with APPARATUS and EXPOSITION, kept lean. That the practice
brings forth concrete works is not a hoped-for side effect; it is §1. Frank's gate
(§2.3) is unchanged.

## §8 Records — proportionate

SCORE as living map (a page, revised); TRACE in proportion to consequence; DECISION
where something is decided. The heavier v4 apparatus (mandate matrices, per-project
kill-condition engineering, §5.4 non-replaceability testing) is retired. Corrections
preserve the record: nothing public is ever silently rewritten or deleted.

## §9 The probation, adopted

The trial `2026-07-24-put-back-on-the-map` reaches its outcome in the strongest
form: its elements are adopted as constitution — the pre-opening check (§4), the
five topoi (§5), and the four danger indicators (format hardening, self-appointed
judge, self-canonisation, kill-grinding) as the standing risk vocabulary of the
monthly review. The probation project is **revised, not killed**: it observes the
adopted instruments in vivo on the first work-line and closes with a balance that
documents this path (adoption through constitutionalisation; deformation record as
evidence for the published work's practicability chapter).
